About Me

I am a Graduate Researcher in the ARIES Lab at Purdue University, specializing in Vision for Robotics. My research focuses on neuromorphic perception, uncertainty-aware sensor fusion for autonomous robot navigation, and 3D scene-aware Vision-Language Model agents that bridge spatial reasoning and natural language understanding for real-world autonomous systems.

Previously, I was a Design Engineer at STMicroelectronics in the Advanced Systems Research (ASR) group, where I worked on R&D for Edge AI applications, focusing on robotics and vision.
